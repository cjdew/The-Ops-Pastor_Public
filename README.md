# The Ops Pastor

Church operations from the inside.

I'm Chris Dewar — Executive Pastor at a 700-person church in Northeast Ohio. I carry executive responsibility for operations, finance, technology, facilities, HR, communications, and data analytics with a team of 10.

I write about building and running operational infrastructure in a context where the budget is small, the team is lean, and the mission matters too much to let it rot.
